I’m Vikram Joshi—an author, dirtrider, climber, and lifelong explorer. This site is where I share my photographs, essays, and ideas shaped by decades of experience in technology and the outdoors. Through images and words, I capture light, stories, and perspectives that explore balance, power, and purpose. Here you’ll find selections from my photography, writings, and ongoing book project. From climbing granite walls in Yosemite to building technology in Silicon Valley, my journey has always been about pushing limits. This site brings together my photography, writing, and research into the stories that matter most.
